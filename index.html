ABOUT QUIZZINGA


Quizzinga!- The Quiz club of KIET takes pride in being one of the most proactive and popular clubs among the students of KIET, Ghaziabad. Established at the backdrop of a highly successful event ‘TechCheck’ , for the college’s tech fest in 2014, the club has grown tremendously and has gathered a huge fan base among the students. The main objective of this club is to build the quizzing culture among students and to encourage their interest in general knowledge awareness and to develop the curiosity of knowing things in them which is the characteristic feature of connecting students with the quizzes. To implement this objective, this club organizes different quizzing events throughout the year on different topics like History & Politics, Entertainment, General, Literature, Sports. In 2016, the club had conducted its first Inter-college quizzing fest Prabuddha. Each of our quizzes till now had an active participation of students and we at Quizzinga encourage their participation with certificates and exciting prizes to the winners and thus implementing our objective.
